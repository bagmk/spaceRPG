Privacy Policy — Cosmic Coalescence

Effective date: June 7, 2026 · Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Saesun Kim ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play Cosmic Coalescence (the "Game") on the web, iOS, and Android.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google or Sign in with Apple, we receive your email address and, if available, your name and profile photo. With Apple you may choose to hide your email (Apple private relay).
  • Profile. The in-game display name you choose.
  • Device identifier. A generated device ID used to save and sync your game progress across sessions and devices.
  • Game data. Your progress, settings, and scores, stored in our cloud backend (Google Firebase / Firestore).
  • Leaderboard data. Your chosen display name and score are visible to other players on public leaderboards.
  • Push notifications (optional). If you enable notifications, a device push token (Firebase Cloud Messaging) is stored to deliver them.
  • Advertising identifier. On iOS (with your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt), we access the IDFA; on Android, the Google Advertising ID (GAID). These are used by Google AdMob to serve and measure rewarded ads. You may opt out at any time in device settings.
  • Purchase history. If you make an in-app purchase, the platform (Apple App Store or Google Play) processes the payment. We receive a confirmation of the purchase (item type, timestamp) to unlock content, but do not receive your payment card details.
  • Diagnostic data. Basic error and performance logs to keep the Game running.

2. How we use information

To provide and maintain the Game; authenticate you; save and sync progress; operate leaderboards; send push notifications you opt into; serve and measure rewarded ads (via Google AdMob); process and fulfill in-app purchases; provide support; and protect against fraud, abuse, and security incidents.

3. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

We process data to perform our contract with you (providing the Game), based on your consent (e.g. optional notifications), and for our legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention). You may withdraw consent at any time.

4. Sharing and service providers

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with the following service providers who process it on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Firestore (cloud save), Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
  • Google AdMob — serves rewarded ads; may collect advertising identifiers and ad interaction data per Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing for in-app purchases; they receive your payment information directly

Your display name and score are shown publicly on leaderboards. We may disclose data if required by law.

5. Tracking and advertising

The Game shows optional rewarded ads (watch a short ad to earn in-game bonuses) powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your advertising identifier to serve relevant ads and measure their effectiveness. On iOS, we request permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before accessing the IDFA; you can deny or revoke this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. On Android, you can opt out via Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID. We do not share your personal game data with advertisers for targeting purposes beyond what AdMob receives for ad serving.

6. Data storage and international transfers

Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure and may be processed in countries other than yours, including the United States, with appropriate safeguards.

7. Retention

We keep your account and game data until you delete your account, after which it is deleted as described below (subject to limited legal retention).

8. Your rights

Depending on your location (e.g. GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, Korea PIPA) you may have the right to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict processing of your data, and to object or withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact saysoonkim@gmail.com.

9. Account and data deletion

You can delete your account and associated data at any time from within the Game (Settings → Delete account). You can also request deletion at /delete-account.html or by emailing saysoonkim@gmail.com. Deletion removes your profile, saved game, and leaderboard entries.

10. Children

The Game is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (under 14 for users in Korea). If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be highlighted in the Game.

12. Contact

게임 제공·운영, 본인 인증, 진행 상황 저장·동기화, 리더보드 운영, 동의한 푸시 알림 발송, Google AdMob을 통한 리워드 광고 제공 및 측정, 인앱 구매 처리, 고객 지원, 부정 이용 방지 및 보안.

회사는 개인정보를 판매하지 않습니다. 회사를 대신해 정보를 처리하는 수탁자는 다음과 같습니다.
