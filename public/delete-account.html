Delete your account & data — Cosmic Coalescence

Published by Saesun Kim (Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA).

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

Open Cosmic Coalescence → Settings → Delete account, then confirm. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — By request

Email saysoonkim@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account, with the subject "Account deletion request". We verify ownership and complete deletion within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Your account, profile (email, display name, photo), saved game progress, settings, device identifier, and leaderboard entries. We may retain minimal records only where required by law.

See our Privacy Policy for details.
